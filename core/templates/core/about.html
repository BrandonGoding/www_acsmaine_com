Our Story

Welcome to ACS Electrical Contractors, your trusted source for expert electrical solutions in Auburn, Maine, and the surrounding areas. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for innovation, we are your go-to electrical partner for residential and commercial electrical projects.

Established in the heart of Auburn, ACS Electrical Contractors has been serving our local community for 20+ years, earning a reputation for reliability, professionalism, and top-tier electrical services. Our journey began with a vision to provide high-quality electrical solutions that enhance the safety, efficiency, and functionality of homes and businesses alike.

Our Mission

At ACS Electrical Contractors, our mission is simple: to empower our clients with safe, reliable, and cost-effective electrical solutions. Whether you're a homeowner or a business, we are dedicated to exceeding your expectations and delivering results that stand the test of time.
